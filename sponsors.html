Sponsors & support

Funding motors, materials, and field logistics multiplies what students can learn. All language here should be vetted with your advisor and school foundation rules.

Why partner with us

Exposure to disciplined STEM work, visible community impact, and students who learn professional habits early — documentation, safety culture, and peer mentorship.

Ways to help

  • In-kind: materials, tool loans, launch equipment
  • Financial: routed through school-approved channels only
  • Expertise: guest briefings (propulsion, structures, program management)
Tax wording: Only repeat statements explicitly approved by your PTA, booster club, or district finance office.

Recognition

Logo placement tiers (website, launch banner, ARC travel shirts) belong in a one-page sponsor packet you co-author with officers — keep this page simple until agreements exist.
