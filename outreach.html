Community House Middle School

High school mentors introducing middle schoolers to rocketry basics — this program is coming soon. Check back here or on Band for updates once CHMS and advisors finalize schedule and sign-up details.

What we’re planning

When the program launches, expect sessions built around range safety, how rockets work, stable flight, and simple low-power builds — not max-altitude bravado. Interest forms and partner contacts will appear on this page after school and club approvals.
